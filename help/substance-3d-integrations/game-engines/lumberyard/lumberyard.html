Lumberyard

The Substance Engine works in Lumberyard Packaged Builds for Windows, Mac, PS4, and Xbox One. The Substance plugin is also compatible with all versions of the Lumberyard Editor (Windows and Mac).

The plugin is natively integrated in the the engine.
