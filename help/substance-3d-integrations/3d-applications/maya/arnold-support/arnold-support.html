Arnold Support

With version 2.2.1, the Substance Outputs can now be directly connected to Arnold Shaders for rendering.

In the Substance Settings, Enable Rendering Extensions for use with Arnold.

Note

Arnold Versions Supported

2017 – MtoA 3.1.0/Arnold 5.2.0

2018 – MtoA 4.0.0/Arnold 6.0.0, MtoA 4.2.0/Arnold 6.2.0

2019 – MtoA 4.0.0/Arnold 6.0.0, MtoA 4.2.0/Arnold 6.2.0, MtoA 5.0.0/Arnold 7.0.0

2020 – MtoA 4.0.0/Arnold 6.0.0, MtoA 4.2.0/Arnold 6.2.0, MtoA 5.0.0/Arnold 7.0.0

2022 – MtoA 4.2.1/Arnold 6.2.0, MtoA 5.0.0/Arnold 7.0.0

2023 - MtoA 4.2.1/Arnold 6.2.0, MtoA 5.0.0/Arnold 7.0.0, MtoA 5.1.0/Arnold 7.1.0, MtoA 5.2.0/ Arnold 7.1.3.
