# Cinema 4D

>[!NOTE]
>
> The Substance plugin is natively installed in Cinema 4D R18 and higher and developed by Maxon.

The Substance plugin basically consists of two components: **Substance files** and a **Substance shader**.

* Substance assets represent the actual Substances within Cinema 4D. They are managed in the Substance Asset Manager.
* Substance shader connects output channels of Substance assets with Cinema 4D material channels.
