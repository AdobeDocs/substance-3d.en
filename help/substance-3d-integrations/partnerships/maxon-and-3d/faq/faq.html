Adobe Substance 3D and Maxon One FAQ

After you complete your purchase of the Maxon One + the Adobe Substance 3D Collection, you will receive a coupon code that allows you to redeem the Substance 3D Collection on the Adobe website with your Adobe ID. If you do not have an Adobe ID, you will be required to create an account and log in to redeem the coupon.

To redeem the Adobe Substance 3D Collection coupon code:

  1. Go to https://redeem.adobe.com.
  2. Sign in or create an Adobe account.
  3. Enter the redemption code that was sent to you and click "Redeem code".
  4. Click "Add payment method" for auto-renewal, or "Remind me later".
  5. Click "Get started".

The coupon code is limited to a single use, and is valid for one year from the date of purchase.

The bundle is a one-time purchase and the special pricing is only available for new subscriptions.

The bundle includes a one year subscription to Maxon One and the Adobe Substance 3D Collection.

  • Maxon One includes:
    • Cinema 4D
    • Redshift
    • Red Giant
    • Zbrush
    • Forger
    • The complete library of Capsule assets
  • The Adobe Substance 3D Collection includes:
    • Substance 3D Modeler
    • Substance 3D Sampler
    • Substance 3D Designer
    • Substance 3D Painter
    • Substance 3D Stager
    • 50 3D assets per month from Substance 3D Assets

Your Maxon One subscription will renew automatically for another year at the normal price ($1199 USD per year, or regional equivalent).

The Adobe Substance 3D Collection will only auto-renew if you enter a valid credit card when you redeem your coupon code, or if you add a credit card to your account management page at account.adobe.com prior to the subscription term expiration date. 

Compare Adobe subscription plans to verify the list price for the Adobe Substance 3D Collection Individual Plan, annual subscription paid upfront.
