# Adobe Substance 3D and Maxon One FAQ

+++How do I get access to the Adobe Substance 3D Collection?
After you complete your purchase of the Maxon One + the Adobe Substance 3D Collection, you will receive a coupon code that allows you to redeem the Substance 3D Collection on the Adobe website with your Adobe ID. If you do not have an Adobe ID, you will be required to create an account and log in to redeem the coupon.

+++

+++How can I redeem the Adobe Substance 3D Collection coupon code?
To redeem the Adobe Substance 3D Collection coupon code:

* Go to <https://redeem.adobe.com>.

* Sign in or create an Adobe account.

* Enter the redemption code that was sent to you and click "Redeem code".

* Click "Add payment method" for auto-renewal, or "Remind me later".

* Click "Get started".

+++

+++How long is the coupon code valid?
The coupon code is limited to a single use, and is valid for one year from the date of purchase.

+++

+++I am an existing Maxon one user. Can I upgrade to the bundle?
The bundle is a one-time purchase and the special pricing is only available for new subscriptions.

+++

+++How do I renew after the first year?
Your Maxon One subscription will renew automatically for another year at the normal price ($1199 USD per year, or regional equivalent).

The Adobe Substance 3D Collection will only auto-renew if you enter a valid credit card when you redeem your coupon code, or if you add a credit card to your account management page at account.adobe.com prior to the subscription term expiration date.

[Compare Adobe subscription plans](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/plans.html) to verify the list price for the Adobe Substance 3D Collection Individual Plan, annual subscription paid upfront.

+++

+++For how long will this bundle be available?
This special bundle is available for a limited time.

+++

+++Does this bundle signal closer collaboration between Adobe Substance 3D  and Maxon?
Both Maxon and Adobe are committed to working together to enhance the creative process for users worldwide. Through this bundle, they continue to demonstrate their dedication to delivering tools that empower artists to transcend boundaries, explore new realms, and craft visuals that resonate with audiences.
