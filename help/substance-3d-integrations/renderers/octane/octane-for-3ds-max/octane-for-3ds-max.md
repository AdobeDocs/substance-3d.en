# Octane for 3ds Max

## Substance in 3ds Max Plugin

Using the[ 3ds Max plugin](../../../3d-applications/3ds-max/3ds-max.md), you can choose Octane in the Substance menu to automatically setup the Octane material with Substance texture inputs.
