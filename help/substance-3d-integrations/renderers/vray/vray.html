Vray

For rendering with Vray you can use maps exported from Substance Painter or the Substance plugin in Maya or 3ds Max.

Substance Painter 2020.1 (6.1.0) ships with VrayMtl shaders for both metallic and specular workflows and supports Vray through Vray Next Output templates.
