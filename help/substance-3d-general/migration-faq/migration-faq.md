+++

+++ When is the move happening?

All of the content being moved is already available on Experience League. The HelpX pages will be updated to automatically redirect to the equivalent Experience League page after March 14th 2026. 

+++

+++ What will happen to my HelpX bookmarks?

On March 14th 2026, HelpX pages for the affected documentation will be updated to automatically redirect to the equivalent Experience League page. As a result, you do not need to update your bookmarks, they will automatically redirect you to the same content on Experience League.

+++

+++ What is Experience League?

Experience League is a documentation platform for Adobe products that includes some powerful search and navigation tools, bringing together documentation, tutorials, and community platforms.
